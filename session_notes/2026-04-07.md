### Key algorithmic insight:
The minimum λ for valid P̃ is λ = max_i {(P₀[i] - Q[i])/P₀[i] : Q[i] < P₀[i]}, not λ = TV(Q, P₀)

### Theoretical discussion:
- TV distance is convenient but other metrics could work with modified proofs
- Sampling is NOT uniform over TV ball (it's Dirichlet-induced)
- Min/max converge to geometric extrema (distribution-free)
- Interior quantiles converge to Dirichlet-measure quantiles (distribution-dependent)
