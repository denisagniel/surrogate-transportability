## Key Insights

### 1. Hierarchy Matters
Not all functionals are equal. The slides make clear that correlation is THE functional, with R-squared and MSPE as brief examples. Alternative functionals (concordance, PPV, NPV, conditional mean) should be marked experimental.

### 2. Paradigm Distinction
CATE covariance represents a fundamentally different paradigm (within-study heterogeneity) than the across-study functionals that are the focus of the method.

### 3. Computational vs Conceptual
Type-level discretization is computational optimization (9x, 487x speedups), not a conceptual component. Keep as internal utilities.

### 4. Inference Approach
Slides present frequentist functional delta method. Bayesian bootstrap is an alternative, not the method.
