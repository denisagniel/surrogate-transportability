### 3. Compositional Shift (Slide 16)

**File:** `slide16_compositional_shift.png` (157KB)

**Content:**
- Two panels (top and bottom)
- Top: Side-by-side histograms of P₀(X) vs Q(X)
  - Shows different proportions across 4 types
  - Color-coded: P₀ (yellow), Q (blue)
- Bottom: Effect functions Δ(X) (constant across types)
  - Single histogram showing same effects for both P₀ and Q
  - Annotation: "Same for P₀ and Q"

**Design:**
- Clearly shows "what changes" vs "what stays constant"
- Emphasizes compositional reweighting concept
- Two-panel layout for comparison

### 4. X-Level vs Observation-Level Schematic (Slide 18) ⭐

**File:** `slide18_xlevel_vs_obslevel.png` (214KB)

**Content:**
- Side-by-side comparison (high priority figure)
- Left panel (X-Level):
  - 4 discrete types shown as circles
  - P₀ (top row) vs Q (bottom row)
  - Arrows showing reweighting
  - Different circle sizes represent different proportions
- Right panel (Observation-Level):
  - 50 individual points as scatter plot
