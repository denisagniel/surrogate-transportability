## Requirements

### MUST Have (Non-Negotiable)

- [ ] Estimand and identification assumptions explicit
- [ ] Theory: characterization (when appropriate) or clear articulation of why classical guarantees are unnecessary
- [ ] Simulations include regimes where the method should struggle (stress-testing)
- [ ] Software: reliable, reproducible; another researcher could apply it correctly
- [ ] Failure modes documented

### SHOULD Have (Preferred)

- [ ] Nuisance vs target performance separated in simulations
- [ ] No estimator without uncertainty quantification unless justified
- [ ] APIs reflect statistical structure; safe defaults

### MAY Have (Optional)

- [ ] Adversarial simulations; behavior under misspecification
- [ ] Evidence of decision impact
