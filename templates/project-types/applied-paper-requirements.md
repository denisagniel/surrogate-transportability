## Requirements

### If full paper

**MUST:** Research question clear; data and methods appropriate; evidence hierarchy respected; limitations stated; claim strength matches evidence; target venue identified.

**SHOULD:** Conceptual clarity; evidence-of-impact where relevant; reproducibility.

### If your sections only

**MUST:** Your sections clear, self-contained, consistent with the rest of the paper; claims scoped to your contribution (no overstating what full paper/team has shown); limitations stated for your part; communication standards (precise language, match claim to evidence).

**SHOULD:** Align with paper’s overall evidence hierarchy; reproducibility for your code/analysis.
